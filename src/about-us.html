Winston Quek

Chief Executive Officer

Winston is a seasoned banking and capital markets professional with more than 26 years of experience with proven track record and management history in global financial markets and corporate banking covering Asia Pacific region. Strong understanding and network of regional clients and broad knowledge of how to bridge non-Asian clients into the region and vice versa. Direct oversight in implementing, rebuilding and enhancing policies in risk, compliance, operations for capital markets and corporate lending and overall regional strategy and business development.

Kris Hopkins

Head of Capital Markets,
APAC

Kris has over 20 years experience in investment markets across market making, high-touch trading, electronic trading, client acquisition and relationship management. Kris spent several years at JPMorgan, is a former Head of Client Execution for South Asia at Merrill Lynch and was Asia Pacific head of eSales for Citibank’s listed derivatives group.

He has a strong pedigree in managing and developing risk governance procedures and has a wide client coverage network comprising of hedge funds, asset managers, sovereign wealth funds, central banks, banks and market makers.

Danny Surtani

CChief Financial Officer

Danny brings more than 25 years of experience as a CFO and Finance Director in the financial services and banking industry. He was the former Asia Pacific CFO for the Investment Bank at Deutsche Bank AG Singapore where he was the business partner on delivery of the financial strategy. He has extensive experience in all aspects of Finance from product control, legal entity control, management reporting, strategy, business performance assessment and reporting, planning, internal control oversight and cost management. He was in Japan with Deutsche Securities and Credit Suisse Financial Products in their Finance areas prior to coming to Singapore. Prior to that he was in London with Ernst & Young in their corporate finance due diligence and audit teams, and is a ICAEW UK Chartered Accountant.

Tom Menner

Chief Technology Officer

Tom is the Chief Technology Officer for SBI Digital Markets. Prior to joining SBI DM he was the Head of Asia Pacific for IntellectEU, technology services for blockchain, confidential computing, and artificial intelligence. He was also the Director of Solution Architecture for R3, a blockchain and digital assets platform company; and he had a lengthy 20+ year career at IBM as a Solution Architect.

Claudia Loh

Chief Operating Officer

Claudia Loh, Chief Operating Officer at SBI Digital, brings over 25 years of experience in Singapore, Taiwan and Hong Kong across senior leadership roles in digital and investment banking at leading global firms. Claudia led the effort to obtain Nordea’s bank license from MAS as deputy GM, developed a long term technology strategy and rebuilt the support of bond trading at Barclays Capital. Claudia led the migration of functions from London to Singapore within the investment banking division at Royal Bank of Scotland, and was a developing member as Head of Client Experience at SC Ventures, the digital bank of Standard Chartered. Claudia spent 7 years at IBM Consulting delivering high profile projects for Asian multinational corporations. Claudia graduated with a Bachelor of Business degree from Nanyang Technological University and is certified by the prestigious Asian Financial Leaders Programme.

Michael Chan

Chief Compliance Officer

Michael brings more than 15 years of experience in the field of risk management, legal and compliance in the financial industry. He was formerly with Temasek, Norges Bank Investment Management and was Head of Compliance for Pareto Securities Asia, where he managed end-to-end compliance and operational risk functions. He has extensive experience working in compliance in the global capital markets and private markets space, building compliance and control programs from the ground up, liaising with global regulators, developing compliance systems, putting in place preventive, detective controls and performing risk assessments to manage compliance, financial crime and operational risk in the APAC region. He is a Certified Compliance Specialist, Anti-Money Laundering Specialist and a member of the Singapore Corporate Counsel Association.
