Our Vision

The Sḵwx̱ wú7mesh Úxwumixw is s7eḵwí7tel, ú xwumixw, and stélmexw. We honour our ancestors by continuing their work for Sḵwx̱ wú7mesh Stélmexw to have a better quality of life than the previous generation. We use, create, and borrow the tools we need for our people to care for themselves and their loved ones. We offer knowledge of our ancestors to create ha7lh sḵwálwen and nexwniw̓ that can be passed on to our descendants.
